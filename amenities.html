Our Amenities

Juice Bar

Refresh and rejuvenate your senses with a delightful array of fresh juices at our on-site Juice Bar. Nestled within Riverside Hotel, our Juice Bar offers a vibrant and inviting atmosphere where you can indulge in a selection of handcrafted beverages made from the finest seasonal fruits and vegetables. From revitalizing green juices packed with nutrients to refreshing fruit blends bursting with flavor, our Juice Bar provides a diverse range of options to suit every palate and preference. Whether you're in need of a morning pick-me-up or a post-workout refreshment, our expertly crafted juices are sure to energize and invigorate you.

Restaurant

Embark on a culinary journey and indulge in gourmet dining experiences at our renowned restaurant, nestled within Riverside Hotel. Our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts, offering a tantalizing selection of international cuisines crafted with the finest ingredients and culinary expertise. From savory delights to sweet temptations, our menu features an array of delectable dishes inspired by flavors from around the world. Whether you're craving traditional Scottish specialties, exotic Asian fusion cuisine, or classic European fare, our talented chefs are dedicated to creating memorable dining experiences that delight the senses and satisfy the palate.

Spa and Wellness

Indulge in pure relaxation and rejuvenation with the luxurious spa services offered at Riverside Hotel. Escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse yourself in a tranquil oasis where you can unwind, recharge, and pamper yourself from head to toe. Our spa boasts a comprehensive range of treatments designed to cater to your every need and desire. From soothing massages that melt away tension and stress to revitalizing facials that leave your skin glowing and radiant, our experienced therapists are committed to providing a personalized experience tailored to your specific preferences and concerns.

Other Facilities

At Riverside Hotel, we offer a plethora of additional facilities to enhance your stay and cater to your every need. Whether you're looking to stay active, relax by the pool, or catch up on work, our hotel has you covered. Stay in shape and energized during your stay with our state-of-the-art fitness center, equipped with modern exercise equipment and amenities to help you maintain your workout routine while away from home. Our dedicated staff is on hand to assist you with any fitness needs or questions you may have. Unwind and soak up the sun at our sparkling swimming pool, where you can enjoy a refreshing dip or simply lounge poolside with a good book and a cool drink in hand. Our pool area provides the perfect retreat for relaxation and leisure, allowing you to escape the stresses of everyday life and immerse yourself in tranquility.
