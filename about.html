About Us

Nestled in the heart of Dundee, Scotland, Riverside Hotel stands as a beacon of luxury and hospitality, offering guests a sanctuary of comfort and elegance along the picturesque riverside. From our prime location to our dedicated team, every aspect of our hotel is designed to ensure an unforgettable experience for our guests.

With its scenic surroundings and tranquil ambiance, Riverside Hotel provides a serene retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Whether you're admiring the breathtaking views of the river from your room or strolling along the riverside promenade, you'll find yourself immersed in the beauty and tranquility of our surroundings.

At Riverside Hotel, hospitality is more than just a profession—it's a passion. Our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional service, going above and beyond to ensure that every guest feels welcomed, valued, and cared for. From the moment you arrive until the moment you depart, you'll be treated to personalized attention and genuine hospitality that will leave a lasting impression.

Since our establishment, Riverside Hotel has been synonymous with luxury and sophistication. Our beautifully appointed rooms offer a perfect blend of style and comfort, providing a peaceful haven where you can relax and rejuvenate. Meanwhile, our exquisite dining options showcase the best of Scottish cuisine, with fresh, locally sourced ingredients prepared with skill and creativity.

But it's not just our accommodations and dining options that set us apart. Riverside Hotel also boasts a range of top-notch amenities designed to cater to your every need. Whether you're looking to unwind with a spa treatment, stay active in our fitness center, or host a business meeting in our state-of-the-art conference facilities, we have everything you need to make your stay with us truly memorable.

Whether you're here for a weekend getaway, a business trip, or a special occasion, Riverside Hotel promises to exceed your expectations at every turn. Come and experience the epitome of luxury and hospitality at Riverside Hotel—where every stay is an unforgettable experience..
